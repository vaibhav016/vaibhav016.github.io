  • Feature Representaion and Integration in Deep acoustic wave models (2021)

    Worked under Dr Vinayak Abrol(IIIT-Delhi) as a research assistant

    This work focussed on understanding how temporal and spectral features are learnt by deep convolutional models. A thorough investigation is made for feature representation by a deep encoder when a raw waveform is provided versus a preprocessed spectrogram. In this study, we hypothesize that low rank temporal filtering over deep convolutional encoder for a raw waveform gives a comparative result as to the state of the art. The work is still in progress.
